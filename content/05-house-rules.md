# House rules

A few simple things to keep the place lovely for you and the next guests.

- **Quiet hours:** please keep things down between **10:00 pm and 7:00 am**, in line with Costa Mesa's noise ordinance.
- **No smoking** anywhere on the premises.
- **Pets:** please check with us first if you'd like to bring one.
- **Guests:** occupancy is as booked — please no extra overnight guests.
- **Larger gatherings:** planning a get-together, especially one running later into the evening? Please **let us know in advance**.

!!! note "Why it matters"
    This is a quiet residential street, so a little consideration goes a long way with the neighbours — and helps us keep welcoming guests here.
