# Make the most of it

This house is meant to be **enjoyed** — the fireplaces, the grills, the hot tub, the steam shower, the patio. Please use them all and have a wonderful time.

The **safety notes** dotted through this guide aren't here to put you off; they're just so everyone has a great, worry-free stay. Skim them once, then relax and enjoy.

### A few local ideas to get you started

- **Lamb chops on the Egg:** grab lamb chops from **Trader Joe's** and lump charcoal from **The Butchery**, bring the Big Green Egg up to about **600°F**, rub the chops in **olive oil and thyme**, and grill **on both sides until browned but not burnt** — if a flame starts to develop, **drop the upper lid** on the Egg to settle it.
- **A morning out:** breakfast at **Plums** or **Breakfast Republic**, both within walking distance. For something scenic, **Driftwood Café** in Laguna Beach sits right over the ocean and the sand.
- **Lunch:** **Zinque** in **Lido Marina Village** — excellent French-infused food with a marina and harbour view, and lovely shopping right there.
- **An upscale evening:** for something special, reserve dinner at **The Dock**.
- **A sunrise worth setting an alarm for:** head to **Dory's Fish Market** (by the Newport Pier) early for sunrise and fresh fish and shellfish straight off the boats. Come at **6 am sharp** or the good stuff is gone — the **Black Cod** is the one to get.
