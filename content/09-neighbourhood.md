# The neighbourhood

A few more of our favourites close by. For a suggested morning, lunch, or evening out, see *Make the most of it*.

### Provisions & wine
- **The Butchery** — high-end cuts and quality meat (plus cheeses, wine, and ready-to-grill options). A few minutes away at **103 E 17th St, Costa Mesa**. Open daily.
- **Hi-Time Wine Cellars** — a Costa Mesa institution for wine. They run **wine tastings** — **reserve ahead**.

### Eat & drink
- **Oak and Coal** — a great local spot for dinner.
- **Miligram** — good for lunch, or for dinner events.

### A walk
- **Around the Back Bay** (Upper Newport Bay) — an easy, scenic loop along the water, close by.
