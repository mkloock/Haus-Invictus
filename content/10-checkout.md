# Before you go

**Check-out is by 11:00 am** — and that's very nearly all we ask.

A **full professional clean is already included in your stay**, so please don't lift a finger tidying. Leave everything to us and enjoy a slow last morning.

### Relax — the cleaning is on us
- **Leave the beds as they are** — no stripping, no remaking.
- **Leave the dishes** — no need to wash up or run the dishwasher.
- **Leave the grills, kitchen, and floors** — our team takes care of all of it.
- **Leave the trash and recycling** — we'll sort it.

### Just a quick safety round
Two minutes before you head out, so nothing's left running:

- **Turn off all the gas** — the indoor fireplaces, the fire pit and tabletop fireplace, and the Lion grill at its supply valve. Make sure the **Big Green Egg's vents are closed**.
- **Switch off the stove and oven**, and turn off the **steam shower**.
- **Turn off the taps** and check nothing is left running.
- Set the **thermostat to a moderate setting** — please don't leave the heating or AC running hard.
- **Close and lock the windows and doors**, including the patio door, and pull the front door firmly shut behind you (the keypad locks it).
- **Take your belongings** — one last look in the master bath, the closets, and out on the patio.

Thank you for staying at **Haus Invictus**. If you enjoyed it, a kind review means a great deal to us.
